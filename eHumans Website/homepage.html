The eHumans guild is a guild filled with Minecraft nerds, pvp pros and gamers all alike. This is a chill place and we are chill people looking to spend good time with each other and have fun. In here, you can make friends and meet people and talk with them and play games and chat with each other! Anyone is welcome, even if you don't play Minecraft. There are people who play other games/have different interests and you can do whatever you want!

How to join the eHumans guild?

Heya People , This is how to join our guild!


➤ Requirements -
As the guild is still in its growing stage , we dont have any specific requirements , only thing we expect from you is to be active! As we increase in size, these requirements will be updated!

➤ How to join? -

  1. To join this guild , you can either make a ticket or DM any staff and we will tell you what to do and when to come online so that we can accept you.
  2. You can execute the command below in Hypixel, and any online staff will accept you in the guild. The Hypixel command is:
    /g join eHumans

➤Things to keep in mind -

  • If your behaviour is exceeding limits, action shall be taken against you.
  • If you are insanely inactive, you will be removed from the guild.
  • Prohibitory actions will be taken against you if you do not follow the Guild/ Hypixel rules. And you will be removed from the Guild.
